# Cod Burger Mod (鳕鱼堡模组)

## Introduction

Cod Burger Mod is a small Minecraft food expansion mod that adds a brand-new food item: **Cod Burger**.
The mod allows players to craft a delicious burger using cod, bringing a fun and immersive cooking experience into the Minecraft world.

Whether you are fishing by the ocean or building your own restaurant, the Cod Burger gives cod a brand-new purpose.

---

## Features

* Adds a new food item: **Cod Burger**
* Simple and balanced crafting recipe
* Compatible with vanilla survival gameplay
* Lightweight and easy to install

---

## New Item

### Cod Burger

A tasty burger made from fresh cod.

**Properties**

* Food points: 8
* Saturation: 19.2

---

## Crafting Recipe

Craft a Cod Burger using the following ingredients:

Bread
Cooked Cod
Bread

(Vertical arrangement in crafting table)

---

## Installation

1. Install **Minecraft Forge / Fabric** (depending on the mod version).
2. Download the Cod Burger Mod `.jar` file.
3. Place the `.jar` file into your Minecraft `mods` folder.
4. Launch the game and enjoy your new Cod Burger!
